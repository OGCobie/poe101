Understanding Path of Exile Currency

Path of Exile uses a unique barter-based economy with no gold. Instead, currency items serve dual purposes: they modify equipment through crafting and act as trading currency. Chaos Orbs are the standard trading currency, while Divine Orbs represent high-value trades. Values fluctuate based on league economy and demand.

Pick Up Wisely

Don't pick up everything! Focus on currency worth 0.5c or more. Scrolls and low-value currency waste inventory space. Efficiency matters more than hoarding.

Check Current Rates

Currency values fluctuate throughout a league. Use trading websites to check current exchange rates before making large trades. Early league prices differ significantly from late league.

üîÑ

Vendor Recipes

Learn important vendor recipes to convert items into currency. For example: a full set of rare items (all slots, ilvl 60+) vendors for 1-2 Chaos Orbs depending on identification status.

Don't Gamble Early

Avoid using Chaos or higher currency for crafting early in the game. It's more efficient to trade for upgrades. Save valuable currency for endgame crafting or big purchases.

üíé

Quality Before Fusing

Always get items to 20% quality before using Jeweller's or Fusings. Quality significantly improves your chances of getting desired socket results. Use the crafting bench when possible.

Divine Orbs Are Precious

Divine Orbs are the most valuable common currency. Use them only on perfect items where the rolls matter. Most items aren't worth divining. Trade them instead for massive purchasing power.
