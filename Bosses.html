Know Your Enemy

Every act in Path of Exile culminates in a challenging boss fight. Understanding attack patterns, damage types, and positioning is crucial to survival. This guide covers all major campaign bosses from Acts 1-10, with proven strategies to defeat each one.

Act Boss Strategy & Key Mechanics
1
Merveil, the Twisted
Siren corrupted by Virtue Gems
 Phase 1: Avoid her ice spear projectiles and keep moving. She's stationary in this phase.
  • Stay at medium range to dodge ice spears
  • Cold resistance is very helpful
  • Kill summons if they overwhelm you
Phase 2: She transforms and becomes mobile. Watch for water spawns and freezing ground effects.
  • Keep moving to avoid her charging attacks
  • Don't stand in freezing water pools
  • Focus on dodging over raw damage output
2
Vaal Oversoul
Ancient stone construct
 Main Mechanics: Massive slam attacks, rock projectiles, and lightning totems.
  • Watch for the slam animation - move immediately when you see it
  • Destroy lightning totems as soon as they spawn
  • Stay mobile and don't stand directly in front of him
  • Lightning resistance recommended for totem damage
  • His laser beam is telegraphed - dodge to the side
3
Dominus, Ascendant
High Templar of the Eternal Empire
 Phase 1 (Human Form): Uses blood rain, touch of god, and summons.
  • Avoid his melee range - his touch attack deals massive damage
  • Watch for blood rain telegraph and move away
  • Kill or kite his summoned minions
  • Physical damage reduction helps significantly
Phase 2 (Demon Form): Transforms into a massive demon with new attacks.
  • Ground slam creates shockwaves - dodge perpendicular to him
  • Blood projectiles track - keep moving in circles
  • Don't stand in blood rain zones
  • Be patient - this is a long fight, survival over speed
4
Malachai, The Nightmare
Architect of the Cataclysm
 Three Phase Fight: One of the hardest campaign bosses.
  • Phase 1: Avoid tentacle slams and teleport traps, watch for heart explosions
  • Phase 2: Piety assists you - focus fire on Malachai, new attack patterns emerge
  • Phase 3: Triple slam is deadly - dodge multiple directions quickly
  • Hearts that spawn will explode - don't stand near them
  • Max your resistances before this fight
  • Bring at least 3 life flasks and keep them full
  • Don't panic - learn the attack patterns through attempts
5
Kitava, The Insatiable
God of Corruption
 First Major Endgame-Style Boss: Tests your build properly for the first time.
  • Ground slam waves - dodge sideways or jump over with movement skill
  • Heart explosions spawn adds - kill or avoid based on situation
  • His hand slam is telegraphed - watch for the wind-up
  • When he screams, adds spawn - be ready to deal with them
  • Fire resistance is crucial for this fight
  • -30% resistance penalty after defeating him - prepare for Act 6
6
The Brine King
Fallen god of the seas
 Water and Ice Themed: Freeze mechanics are dangerous.
  • He summons minions constantly - decide when to kill vs. kite them
  • Freeze beam is his most dangerous attack - keep moving
  • Water spouts appear on the ground - avoid standing in them
  • Freeze immunity flask is extremely helpful
  • Cold resistance should be capped
  • Stay mobile - standing still = getting frozen
7
Arakaali, Spinner of Shadows
Spider goddess
 Spider Mechanics: Spawns and poison are the main threats.
  • Baby spiders spawn throughout - clear them periodically
  • Web spray slows you - don't get trapped against walls
  • She leaps across the arena - watch for landing spot
  • Projectile spray in a cone - dodge to the sides
  • Poison immunity or removal flask recommended
  • Keep her centered in the arena to avoid being cornered
8
Solaris & Lunaris
Twin goddesses of sun and moon
 Dual Boss Fight: You fight them separately, then together.
  • Solaris uses fire beams and creates burning ground - constant movement required
  • Lunaris fires cold projectiles and creates freezing zones
  • When fighting both: focus one while dodging the other
  • Max both fire and cold resistances
  • This is one of the hardest fights in the campaign
  • Don't be afraid to over-level or upgrade gear for this fight
  • Use pillars for line of sight breaking when overwhelmed
9
The Depraved Trinity
Shavronne, Doedre & Maligaro fused
 Three Bosses in One: Each with signature mechanics.
  • Shavronne: Lightning attacks and teleports - keep distance
  • Doedre: Curses and chaos damage - curse removal flask helps
  • Maligaro: Blade attacks and fast movement - watch for telegraphs
  • They share a health pool but attack in sequence
  • Final combined phase is hectic - prioritize dodging
  • All three resistances should be capped
  • Focus fire one at a time rather than spreading damage
10
Kitava, The Insatiable (Final)
God of Corruption - Empowered
 Final Campaign Boss: All mechanics from Act 5 but more dangerous.
  • All previous Kitava attacks but faster and harder hitting
  • Screen-wide sweep attack - move to designated safe zones
  • When he covers half the arena in corruption - reposition immediately
  • Heart explosions are more frequent - constant awareness needed
  • Ground slams create multiple waves - dodge timing is critical
  • This is a marathon fight - patience is key
  • Bring 4-5 life flasks and a quicksilver for mobility
  • Survival is more important than damage - focus on not dying
  • After defeating: -30% more resistance penalty (total -60%)

Cap Your Resistances

The single most important preparation for any boss. Aim for 75% elemental resistances. After Acts 5 and 10, you'll have penalties that reduce your resistances by 30% each time.

Bring the Right Flasks

Multiple life flasks are essential. Utility flasks for freeze, bleed, and curse immunity can make difficult fights trivial. A quicksilver flask helps with dodging.

Learn the Patterns

Don't be discouraged by deaths. Each attempt teaches you attack patterns. Most boss attacks are telegraphed - watch for animations and visual cues before big hits.

Upgrade Your Gear

Struggling with a boss? Check your gear. Often a weapon upgrade or better life/resistance rolls on armor can make a huge difference. Don't be afraid to trade or craft.

Positioning Matters

Many boss attacks are directional or create ground effects. Stay mobile, use the arena space, and don't get cornered. Movement skills like Dash or Flame Dash can be lifesavers.

Level Up If Needed

There's no shame in being a few levels higher. If a boss is destroying you, go back and level up in earlier zones. Being 2-3 levels higher can make a significant difference.
