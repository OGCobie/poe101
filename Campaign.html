The Campaign Awaits

Path of Exile's campaign spans 10 acts across the dark continent of Wraeclast. You'll face corrupted beasts, fallen gods, and ancient evils as you uncover the truth behind your exile. Each act introduces new mechanics, challenges, and enemies that will test your build and skill. The journey typically takes 6-10 hours for experienced players, longer for newcomers.

Keep Your Gear Updated

Replace gear every 10-15 levels. Prioritize life and resistances on gear. A good weapon makes leveling much smoother.

Cap Your Resistances

Aim for 75% elemental resistances by Act 5. Chaos resistance is less important but helpful. Resistances are penalized after certain acts.

Level Your Gems

Keep your main skill gems in your gear at all times. They gain experience even in your weapon swap. Quality gems provide significant bonuses.

Don't Over-Explore

You don't need to clear every zone. Focus on main objectives and stay within 3-4 levels of the zone. Efficiency matters for progression.

Use Your Flasks

Life and mana flasks are crucial. Upgrade them as you level. Utility flasks (quicksilver, granite) provide massive benefits.

Plan Your Passive Tree

Focus on life nodes and damage for your main skill. A good rule: 200% increased life by endgame. Don't spread too thin early.

Main Objectives

Act Boss
Merveil, the Twisted

A siren corrupted by a magical gem. Fight her in two phases. In phase one, avoid her ice spears. In phase two, she summons water spawns and uses powerful cold attacks. Keep moving and don't stand in her AoE.

The Forest Encampment ‚Ä¢ The Riverways ‚Ä¢ The Wetlands

Venture inland to confront warring bandit lords and uncover ancient Vaal ruins. Act 2 introduces the bandit quest decision that affects your character permanently. You'll explore forests, ruins, and crypts while learning about the Vaal civilization's dark history.

Act Boss
Vaal Oversoul

A massive stone construct from the ancient Vaal empire. It slams the ground with devastating force and fires rock projectiles. Stay mobile, dodge the slams, and watch for his laser beam attack. Kill the lightning totems he summons quickly.

Act Three

The City of Sarn

Sarn Encampment ‚Ä¢ The Slums ‚Ä¢ The Marketplace

Enter the ruins of Sarn, once the capital of the Eternal Empire. Navigate through corrupted city districts, sewers, and the famous Library. Act 3 is significantly longer than previous acts and introduces ribbon-bearing monsters. You'll face multiple boss encounters and unlock your first Labyrinth trial.

Main Objectives

  • Clear the Crematorium and collect Tolman's Bracelet
  • Defeat the Gemling Queen in the Marketplace
  • Find the Sewer Keys and navigate the sewers
  • Kill General Gravicius in the Barracks
  • Complete the Library quest for skill point books
  • Slay Piety at the Lunaris Temple
  • Defeat Dominus at the Sceptre of God
Act Boss
Dominus, Ascendant

The High Templar himself, fought in two phases. Phase one: He uses blood rain, touch of God, and summons. Stay away from his melee attacks. Phase two: He transforms into a demon. Avoid his ground slam, blood projectiles, and don't stand in the rain of blood. Keep your distance and be patient.

Act Four

The Nightmare Realm

Highgate ‚Ä¢ The Dried Lake ‚Ä¢ The Mines

Journey to Highgate at the base of the mountain and confront the Beast's corruption. This act is shorter but more challenging, featuring multiple difficult bosses. You'll delve into nightmare-infested mines and face powerful guardians before confronting Malachai himself.

Main Objectives

  • Defeat Voll in the Dried Lake

  • Slay Kaom, King of Karui in Kaom's Stronghold

Act Boss
Malachai, The Nightmare

The architect of the Cataclysm and master of the Beast. Three-phase fight. Avoid his tentacle slams, teleport traps, and heart explosions. In the second phase, Piety helps you while he gains new attacks. Final phase is the most dangerous - watch for his triple slam and the exploding hearts. Stay mobile and don't panic.

Return to Oriath to find it under siege by Kitava's forces. The streets are filled with the undead and demonic creatures. This act takes you through the ruins of the Templar stronghold and into the heart of the corruption. Be prepared for tough fights and dramatic revelations.

God of Corruption and first major endgame-style boss. He has multiple dangerous attacks: ground slam waves, heart explosions, and adds. Focus on dodging, especially his telegraphed slams. Kill the adds when they overwhelm you. This fight teaches you patterns that matter later.

A fallen god commanding water and ice. He summons minions, fires ice projectiles, and creates freezing ground. His dangerous attacks include a freeze beam and massive water spouts. Keep moving, manage the adds, and don't get frozen. Freeze immunity flasks help tremendously.

Arakaali, Spinner of Shadows

The spider goddess who feeds on suffering. She spawns baby spiders, fires webs that slow you, and leaps across the arena. Her most dangerous attack is a spray of projectiles. Keep moving, clear the small spiders periodically, and dodge when she leaps. Bring poison flasks if possible.

Act Eight

The Gemling Legions

The Sarn Ramparts ‚Ä¢ The Grain Gate ‚Ä¢ The Imperial Fields

Return to Sarn's outskirts to face the Gemling Legion led by Solaris and Lunaris, the sun and moon goddesses locked in eternal conflict. Navigate through war-torn fields, aqueducts, and the Eternal Laboratory. This act features some of the most challenging boss encounters in the campaign.

Main Objectives

  • Navigate the Toxic Conduits and Doedre's Cesspool

  • Collect the Sun and Moon Wings

Act Bosses
Solaris & Lunaris

Twin goddesses representing day and night. You fight them separately, then together. Solaris uses fire beams and creates scorching ground. Lunaris fires cold projectiles and creates freezing zones. When fighting both, focus on one while dodging the other. This is one of the hardest fights in the campaign - bring good gear and max resistances.

Travel through the Vastiri Desert and into the ancient Maraketh territories. Face the Sand King and his armies while navigating through scorching deserts and mysterious ruins. Act 9 is known for the Blood Aqueduct, a popular farming zone, and features multiple tough boss encounters.

Act Boss
The Depraved Trinity

Three fused bosses: Shavronne, Doedre, and Maligaro. Each has their signature attacks. Shavronne fires lightning, Doedre uses curses and chaos damage, Maligaro summons blades. They share a health pool but you fight them in sequence. The combined phase at the end is hectic - focus on dodging and use your flasks wisely.

Act Ten

The Final Confrontation

Oriath Square ‚Ä¢ The Ravaged Square ‚Ä¢ The Feeding Trough

Return to Oriath for the final showdown with Kitava. The city is in ruins, consumed by the god of corruption. Navigate through devastated streets filled with powerful enemies before facing Kitava one last time. Completing Act 10 unlocks the Atlas of Worlds and true endgame content.

Main Objectives

  • Clear the Ravaged Square
  • Navigate the Torched Courts and Desecrated Chambers
  • Defeat Vilenta in the Ossuary
  • Find Bannon's Staff in the Reliquary
  • Kill Avarius in the Canals
  • Confront Kitava for the final time
Final Boss
Kitava, The Insatiable (Final Form)

The ultimate campaign boss with devastating attacks. He slams the ground creating waves, summons hearts that explode, and fires projectile barrages. His most dangerous move is the screen-wide sweep - watch for the animation and move to safe zones. When he covers half the arena in corruption, reposition quickly. This is a marathon fight - stay patient, keep your flasks up, and focus on survival over damage.
