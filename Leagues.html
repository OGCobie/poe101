League History

Path of Exile releases a new challenge league roughly every 13–16 weeks. Each league introduces unique mechanics, expansion content, and balance changes. Here are the last 10 leagues and what they offered.

League Release Content Offered
Keepers Oct 2025 Introduced Keeper NPCs with customizable modifiers and rewards.
Mercenaries Jun 2025 Added mercenary companions with unique skills and contracts.
Settlers of Kalguur Jul 2024 Expansion featuring Kalguur settlers, new crafting lantern mechanic.
Necropolis Mar 2024 Undertaker NPC, corpse crafting system, graveyard encounters.
Affliction Dec 2023 Wildwood areas, Wisps mechanic, new ascendancy classes.
Ancestor Aug 2023 Trial of the Ancestors tournament, new Karui-themed uniques.
Crucible Apr 2023 Forge weapons with passive skill trees, new Atlas changes.
Sanctum Dec 2022 Introduced roguelike Forbidden Sanctum dungeon with relic system.
Kalandra Aug 2022 Lake of Kalandra reflections, mirrored encounters, new uniques.
Sentinel May 2022 Deploy sentinels to empower monsters for loot multipliers.
